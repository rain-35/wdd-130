Ultimate Tic-Tac-Toe is a fun twist on the classic game of Tic-Tac-Toe. Instead of just one grid, you play on a 3x3 grid of smaller Tic-Tac-Toe boards. On your turn, you place your symbol ("X" or "O") on one of the small boards, and that tells your opponent where they have to play next. For example, if you play in the top-left corner of a small board, your opponent must play in the top-left small board of the big grid. You win a small board by getting three symbols in a row, and the goal is to win three small boards in a row on the big grid.
